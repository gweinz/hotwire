How Hotwire works

1. Gather around this deck of cards with your friends.

2. Pick who goes first, it is their turn to play the top card.

3. Play the card. (Descriptions of cards below).

4. Discard. Follow clockwise motion to determine whose turn it is now.

5. Play until a player reaches 22 points. Or any other point total up to you. Also can incorporate drinking by taking a sip for each point you lose and handing out sips for each point you gain.

Types of cards (says at the top)

Hotwire

The person playing this card asks the group where the NFL player on the card went to college. DO NOT SHOW THE OTHER PLAYERS THE CARD. First person to get it right gets to keep the card and thus gain the points. We recommend limiting one guess per person per card and to have people raise hands to establish a queue of answering. Answer is on the bottom.

Reverse

Place the card face up in the middle of the table. Starting with the person playing the card go around in a clockwise motion naming current NFL players from the school that is on the card. The first person to mess up or be wrong keeps the card.
